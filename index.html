About

We are the Gonzaga student chapter of the national ACM organization. We hold club meetings that cover various industry topic throughout the academic year. At our meetings you can find us hosting guest speakers, sharing career advice, providing homework help, and many other things! We also host the annual GU Hackathon in the Fall. Interested in joining or want to have a chat? Head over to our contacts section.
