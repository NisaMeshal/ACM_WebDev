About

We are the Gonzaga student chapter of the national ACM organization. We hold club meetings that cover various industry topic throughout the academic year. At our meetings you can find us hosting guest speakers, sharing career advice, providing homework help, and many other things! We also host the annual GU Hackathon in the Fall. Interested in joining or want to have a chat? Head over to our contacts section.

Events

Leetcode

We often do Leetcode problems together at our meetings. Leetcode is an excellent way to improve the skills employers look for in technical interviews.

Hackathon

Every fall we host a 12-hour hackathon. It's open to everyone and is a fantastic opportunity to build something meaningful and learning something new in a low-stakes environment. Participants have the opportunity to present their creations at the end of the event to a panel of judges and possibly win a prize as well as definitely improve their professional presentation and pitching skills. There is free food and T-shirts for everyone!

Cybercup

https://spokanectf.github.io/
The Spokane Mayors Cup Cybersecurity Competition is held every spring at GU. Students participate in a capture-the-flag style event where they complete challenges to find keys and accumulate points. Free food provided!
