About

  • Who:

    GU Chapter of the Association for Computing Machinery

  • Meetings:

    Meetings involve a wide range of activities spanning from resume workshops and guest speakers from the industry to homework/project help and de-stress events.

  • Campus Involvement:

    We organize an annual county-wide Hackathon (recently we had visitors from WSU as well).

Events

Leetcode

We often do Leetcode problems together at our meetings. Leetcode is an excellent way to improve the skills employers look for in technical interviews.

Hackathon

Every fall we host a 12-hour hackathon. It's open to everyone and is a fantastic opportunity to build something meaningful and learn something new in a low-stakes environment. Participants have the opportunity to present their creations at the end of the event to a panel of judges to improve their professional presentation pitching skills and possibly win a prize. There is free food and T-shirts for everyone!

Cybercup

https://spokanectf.github.io/
The Spokane Mayors Cup Cybersecurity Competition is held every spring at GU. Participants compete in a capture-the-flag style event where they complete challenges to find keys and accumulate points. Free food provided!
